## Key Differences Summary

| Aspect | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Tone** | Formal, polite | Casual, natural |
| **Grammar** | Always perfect | Sometimes imperfect (authentic) |
| **Length** | Consistent 2 sentences | Variable (1-3, sometimes just emoji) |
| **Questions** | Interview-style | Conversational |
| **Personality** | Generic, bland | Distinct, expressive |
| **Language** | English only | Multi-language (Hebrew, Arabic, etc.) |
| **Emojis** | Rare or excessive | Natural, 1-2 per message |
| **Context** | Often forgets | Remembers and references |
| **Variety** | Repetitive patterns | Unique each time |
| **Authenticity** | Sounds like AI | Sounds like a person |
