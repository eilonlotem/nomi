Delete Your Nomi Account & Data

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (name, photos, bio)
  • Your matches and conversations
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Your swipe history
  • All messages you've sent

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Through the App

1. Log in to Nomi
2. Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@nomi.app with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Confirmation that you want all data deleted

Option 3: Facebook Settings

You can also remove Nomi's access through Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings ‚Üí Apps and Websites
  2. Find Nomi and click "Remove"
  3. Select "Delete all posts, photos, and videos on Facebook that Nomi may have published on your behalf"

Data Deletion Timeline

After receiving your request:

  • Immediate: Your profile is hidden from other users
  • Within 24 hours: Your data is marked for deletion
  • Within 30 days: All data is permanently deleted from our servers

You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.
